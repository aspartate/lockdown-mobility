Locked Down: COVID-19 and Nationwide Mobility

.
A visual story on nationwide mobility during COVID-19


In 2020, pandemic-related lockdowns were prevalent across the US. We know that some areas instituted stricter lockdowns than others, and some acted later or not at all.
The question remains: what were the actual effects of these public policies on human behavior?

In other words, how closely did people adhere to lockdown guidelines, and if such guidelines were not present, did mobility patterns change?
Answers to such questions would be relevant to discussions of effective public health policy and may help explain the disparate impact of COVID-19 across the country.
